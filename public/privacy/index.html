Privacy Policy

We collect only what’s needed to run the service. Schools control their tenant data boundaries.

Storefronts live at /s/<school>. App lives at /app.
Data we store
  • Account profile + authentication identifiers
  • School membership + roles
  • Purchase/entitlement records
  • Quiz attempts and progress
Data isolation

Tenant data is scoped by school_id and enforced by the platform tenancy layer.
